Assasin Creed

Assassin's Creed is an action-adventure video game developed by Ubisoft Montreal and released in 2007. It is the first installment in the Assassin's Creed series. The game is set during the Third Crusade in the Holy Land and follows the story of Altaïr Ibn-La'Ahad, a member of the Assassin Brotherhood.

Assassin's Creed IV: Black Flag is an action-adventure video game developed by Ubisoft Montreal and released in 2013. Set during the Golden Age of Piracy in the early 18th century, the game follows the adventures of Edward Kenway, a pirate and member of the Assassin Brotherhood. The narrative blends historical events with fictional elements, featuring famous pirates like Blackbeard and Charles Vane.

Assassin's Creed Syndicate is an action-adventure video game developed by Ubisoft and released in 2015. Set in Victorian-era London during the Industrial Revolution, the game follows twin Assassins, Jacob and Evie Frye, as they fight against the Templars' control over the city.

Assassin's Creed Valhalla is an action role-playing video game developed by Ubisoft Montreal and released in 2020. It is the twelfth major installment in the Assassin's Creed series and follows the journey of Viking warriors as they settle in England during the Dark Ages.

Assassin's Creed II is an action-adventure video game developed by Ubisoft Montreal and released in 2009. It is the second installment in the Assassin's Creed series, set during the Italian Renaissance. The game follows the story of Ezio Auditore da Firenze, a young nobleman who becomes an Assassin after a family tragedy.

Assassin's Creed Rogue is an action-adventure game developed by Ubisoft and released in 2014. Set during the mid-18th century, it bridges the gap between Assassin's Creed III and Assassin's Creed IV: Black Flag. The story follows Shay Patrick Cormac, an Assassin-turned-Templar, providing a unique perspective on the series' usual conflict.

Assassin's Creed Origins is an action-adventure game developed by Ubisoft and released in 2017. Set in Ancient Egypt during the Ptolemaic period (49-47 BC), it serves as a prequel to the entire series, exploring the origins of the Assassin Brotherhood.

In Assassin's Creed Mirage, you are Basim, a cunning street thief with nightmarish visions seeking answers and justice. Join an ancient organisation and come to understand a new creed – one that will change Basim’s fate in ways he never could have imagined. The game was released 2023

Assassin's Creed III is an action-adventure video game developed by Ubisoft and released in 2012. Set during the American Revolution, it follows the story of Connor (Ratonhnhaké ), a half-Mohawk, half-English Assassin fighting against Templar control in the American colonies.

Assassin's Creed Unity is an action-adventure video game developed by Ubisoft and released in 2014. Set during the French Revolution, the game follows Arno Dorian, a young Assassin seeking redemption and uncovering conspiracies in Paris.

Assassin's Creed Odyssey is an action role-playing game developed by Ubisoft and released in 2018. Set in Ancient Greece during the Peloponnesian War (431-422 BC), it allows players to experience the conflict between Athens and Sparta through the eyes of a mercenary.
